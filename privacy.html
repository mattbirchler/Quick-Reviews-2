Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2025

Overview

This privacy policy describes how our app collects, uses, and protects any information when you use our service. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and we collect only the minimum necessary data to provide our services.

Data Collection and Use

Our app is designed with privacy in mind. We collect and transmit very limited data:

  • When using Magic Mode, we only transmit movie titles to the Open Movie Database (OMDB) to retrieve movie metadata
  • All other data and information you input remains locally on your device
  • We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information
  • We do not use cookies or tracking technologies
  • We do not store your search history on our servers

Data Storage

All your app data is stored locally on your device. We do not maintain any servers that store your personal information or usage data.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is the Open Movie Database (OMDB) API. When you enable Magic Mode:

  • Movie titles are sent to OMDB to retrieve movie information
  • This transmission is done securely
  • No personal information is included in these requests

Children’s Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the “Last updated” date.
